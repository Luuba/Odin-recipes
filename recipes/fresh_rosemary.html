Fresh Rosemary Chicken Thighs

Description

Easy baked chicken thigh recipe featuring fresh rosemary and a touch of lemon zest. Bake extra of these thighs and keep in the fridge for up to three days to use in salads and sandwiches.

Ingredients

  • ½ tablespoons chopped fresh rosemary leaves
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 chicken thighs
  • olive oil cooking spray

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Combine rosemary, lemon zest, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Rub over chicken thighs and place in a baking dish. Spray with cooking spray.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until thighs are no longer pink in the centers and juices run clear, 35 to 40 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
